Ordering

  •  Please note that we will need more than 24hrs notice before despatch to you and that delivery days are Tuesday to Friday.
  • We can deliver to your home or place of work.
  • Please provide us with delivery instructions for the courier for example where your parcel could be left should you not be in due to unexpected circumstances. This is also important if this is a gift and you cannot guarantee the recipients whereabouts. "leave behind green bin/next door no25/in porch"

We use a "next day" service and generally this works but things can go awry - weather conditions, breakdowns, wrong sort of snow! So, to avoid unnecessary stress it is best to allow a little bit of slack as far as timing is concerned.

However, if you have an exceptional circumstance do feel free to give us a call and we will do our best to arrange things for you.

 Successful Delivery

To make sure that you receive your package quickly please ensure that your address is full and accurate. If,like us here at the dairy, your postcode leads couriers astray, please put extra instructions for delivery. Also please leave the receivers telephone number as the courier will call if he can't find the correct property.
