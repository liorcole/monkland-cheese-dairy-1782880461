Cheese Tours

Tour the cheese dairy with our cheesemaker. Find out about the process from milk to maturation.

GOOD NEWS, TOURS ARE AVAILABLE AGAIN.

To book go to

www.monklandcheesemakers.co.uk

Here you can book dates and times and pay. All information regarding the tours is there.

We are two separate businesses now but working closely together so please feel free to join us for a pre-tour or post-tour coffee / tea, cake or a cheese led lunch/snack. we look forward to seeing you.

A big thank-you to all our visitors over the past 28 years who have enjoyed the tours and cheese tasting. And, thanks also to all my cheesemaking/tour guides over the years who have explained the delights and mystery of milk becoming cheese. We are still here but Monkland Cheesemakers LTD take over production July 31st 2023.
