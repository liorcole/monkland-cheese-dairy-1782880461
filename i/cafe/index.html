Monkland Cheese Dairy Cafe

Open 10 - 4 Mon-Sat

We have a very simple menu. Our classic offering is the Little Hereford Ploughmans Lunch which is a serving of our Little Hereford cheese, homebaked bread, welsh butter, sweet apple chutney and grape, celery, apple and walnut garnish. We obviously want you to enjoy our cheese while you are here so, cheese platter, blue cheese ploughman, cheese roll.........We also serve our own soup, homemade cakes and during high season our cream tea with clotted cream and Carol makes some lovely strawberry jam.

Our aim is simple, local fare made well.

Our cafe seats 65 on two floors. There is lots of seating in the gardens.

If you are a group we can arrange something that is suitable for you. Just give us a call and see what we can do.
